Before You Reach Out

Think about what kind of horror experience you want. Some players enjoy action horror with weapons and combat, while others prefer slower psychological horror with puzzles, atmosphere, and mystery. Knowing what scares you or interests you can help us recommend better games.

You can also visit the Beginner's Guide page if you are new to the genre and want a simple place to start.
